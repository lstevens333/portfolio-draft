 About Me 

Leah Stevens is a San Jose based artist with a love and passion for Digital Illustration, Graphic Design, and Video Art. She is experienced in many software platforms including Photoshop, Illustrator, Procreate and Premiere Pro/After Effects. Her work covers many topics she is passionate about including environmental issues, mental health, and much more. Leah’s inspiration comes from many sources including her hometown, family, and media.
